Steps to Prepare for an Interview

  1. Research the company and the role you are applying for.
  2. Prepare answers to common interview questions.
  3. Plan your interview attire in advance.
  4. Practice your interview skills with a friend or family member.
  5. Prepare questions to ask the interviewer.
  6. Ensure you have all necessary documents and materials ready.
  7. Arrive early and stay calm during the interview.
